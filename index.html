What it is

A fully local, offline survey array.

HellzGate C5 is a passive, multi-node wireless survey platform. One master coordinates up to nine ESP32-C5 scanner nodes that listen across Wi-Fi and Bluetooth, aggregate to the master, geo-tag, and log — all on your own local network.

  • Passive Wi-Fi (2.4 / 5 GHz) and BLE scanning
  • GPS-tagged, timestamped records exported in WiGLE-ready format
  • Local dashboard + companion app — no cloud, no servers, no account
  • Dedicated wired I²C backplane keeps every radio free for the capture
  • Passive and listen-only, start to finish

Going Independent

Our board. Our firmware. Our direction.

HellzGate is moving to clean-room firmware written from scratch for our own custom hardware — plus a dedicated companion app, both built from the ground up. No more forks. No more someone else's roadmap. Built in phases, with more landing as we hit milestones.

HellzGate started on Piglet, created by Hamspiced. Huge thanks to Ham for the foundation, the inspiration, and the support along the way. From here forward, HellzGate stands on its own.
